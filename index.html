Audio Samples of the paper "Disentangled speaker and language representations using mutual information minimization and domain adaptation for cross-lingual TTS

Authors: Detai Xin, Tatsuya Komatsu, Shinnosuke Takamichi, Hiroshi Saruwatari

Note: In order to obtain best quality, we strongly encourage the listeners to take their headphones.

Contents

  • Intra-lingual TTS synthesis
  • Cross-lingual TTS synthesis

 

We use the combination of VCTK and JVS as training dataset. It includes 107 English and 100 Japanese speakers. We randomly picked 8 Japanese and 8 English speakers as unseen speaker. Audios of unseen speakers were excluded from the training dataset.

All of the utterances below are not included in training data.
